How to Cook Baked Chicken

Description

Need a quick and easy dinner? Try this quick and easy baked chicken thighs recipe! It is simple to make with just a few ingredients and it comes together in about half an hour.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs
  • 4 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 teaspoons onion flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Place chicken thighs in a baking dish; season both sides with garlic powder and onion flakes.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear, about 30 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of thigh, near the bone, should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
